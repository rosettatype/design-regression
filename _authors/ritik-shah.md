Ritik Shah’s interest in wayfinding and multiscript typography stems from his background in information design and his love for cities. He is particularly interested in how typography and spatial design can make developing and organic urban environments more legible and inclusive. After being awarded a Distinction in his MA in Communication Design (Information Design pathway) at the University of Reading, he worked with Applied Information Group in London on various wayfinding and urban environment projects.
